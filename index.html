Interested In Joining?

Studio OneToEight is a small game development studio dedicated to creating unique immersive worlds to explore at your hearts content. We are working on developing games for PC, VR, and even Mobile.

Want to bring life to our projects through your artistic creativity? Become an a member of the team as an artist!

Interested in producing those small sound effects in those tiny details within videogames? Or do you prefer creating soundtracks to fit the mood and complete a story? Either way, you'd be a perfect fit as a member of our team!

Want to express your creativity by contributing to one of the main factors of game development? As a game designer, you can include your ideas for certain game mechanics or features you have in mind, and create something that you and the player will both enjoy!
